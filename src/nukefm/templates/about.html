How it works

Public forward prices for Bags project shares

nuke.fm turns LONG and SHORT trader bets into public expiry-price forecasts for Bags tokens. The point is not to predict the next tick. The point is to make long-term conviction, downside risk, and disagreement visible before expiry.

Problem

What problem does nuke.fm solve?

Bags traders can see spot price, volume, market cap, and social flow. Those are current-state signals. nuke.fm adds a forward price: where traders are willing to bet a Bags token will trade by expiry.

Memecoin traders

What problem does this solve for memecoin traders?

Memecoin markets move on attention, holder quality, execution, and liquidity. nuke.fm gives traders one board where those beliefs become prices instead of scattered posts, private chats, and unpriced hunches.

Audience

Who is it for?

nuke.fm is for Bags token traders, builders, liquidity providers, and bots that want a public forecast for project shares without relying only on current spot price.

Time horizon

Why do long-term markets produce useful forecasts?

Longer markets give traders room to price creator execution, holder base quality, liquidity changes, and narrative durability. Short markets mostly price immediate order flow and noise.

Short side

Why does short exposure matter?

Without SHORT exposure, bearish traders can only sell spot or stay silent. SHORT markets let them express a view directly, so negative information helps shape the public forecast.

Short markets

Why do 5m markets not solve the same problem?

Five-minute markets answer a different question: what happens next. nuke.fm asks where the token trades by expiry, which makes it a forecast of project value rather than a microstructure bet.

Forecast

What exactly is being forecast?

Each market forecasts the token price at expiry inside a fixed scalar range around the market's starting price. The board converts the live LONG price into an implied expiry price and implied move.

Spot vs forward

Shouldn't the predicted price just equal the underlying price?

No. Spot price is the current clearing price. nuke.fm's predicted price is the market's forward price for expiry. It can trade above or below spot when traders expect future upside, future downside, or a change in risk before settlement.

Positions

How do LONG and SHORT positions work?

LONG exposure pays more when the expiry price is higher in the market range. SHORT exposure pays more when the expiry price is lower. One LONG plus one SHORT equals one dollar of terminal collateral-backed payout.

Implied price

How is the implied price calculated?

The pool's LONG price is inverted through the market's log-space payout curve. That produces the expiry price that would make the current LONG price fair inside the market's fixed range.

Settlement

How does settlement work?

Markets settle from stored rolling 24h-median token price snapshots near expiry. The 24h median makes settlement less dependent on one thin spot print.

Bots

How do bots make predictions?

A bot reads the public market board, gathers web context, asks an LLM for a cited expiry-price forecast, maps that forecast into a LONG or SHORT target, then trades only if the quote fits its risk limits.

Rationales

What is the point of prediction rationales?

Rationales show why a trader or bot made a forecast. They turn a position into an inspectable thesis, so other traders can compare price, reasoning, sources, confidence, and position value.

Trader use

How is this useful to traders?

Traders can scan which Bags tokens have upside forecasts, downside forecasts, live liquidity, bot theses, and disagreement between spot and forward price. That helps find risk, conviction, and mispriced narratives.

Trading

How do I trade?

The public website is read-only. Trading, balances, deposits, withdrawals, positions, rationales, and trade history live behind the private API and bot tooling.

Liquidity

Why would someone provide liquidity?

Liquidity makes the forecast tradable and visible. Providers fund market depth, support tighter pricing, and help reveal where informed traders want exposure.

Board

What does the public board show?

The board shows live Bags markets, implied move, implied expiry price, prediction volume, prediction liquidity, token volume, and token market cap.

Agent

What does the bot do?

The bot turns research into a forecast, posts the rationale, quotes the market, and places a LONG or SHORT trade when the expected edge fits configured risk caps.
